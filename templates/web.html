The plot shows percentage of the population vs days since deaths started in each country. This is useful as it tells us which country has been the most affected by the virus,

Acceleration of deaths

The increase of deaths reported per day over the last 7 days as a percentage of the countries population. A positive acceleration means the number of deaths being reported per day is increasing - the situation is getting worse. Countries that are recovering will have a negative acceleration. Countries with a near 0 acceleration have stabilised the situation and should start to recover

Acceleration of confirmed cases

The increase of confirmed cases reported per day over the last 7 days as a percentage of the countries population.
